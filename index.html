About Me

I am a professor of Aeronautical Engineering at the Technical University of Madrid in the Department of Fluid Mechanics and Aerospace Propulsion.

I have a degree in Theoretical Physics and Astrophysics from the University of Valencia and a Ph.D. in Aeronautical Engineering from the Polytechnic University of Madrid.

I teach courses in fluid mechanics and computational fluid dynamics.

My research focuses on hypersonic flows and computational acoustics, aiming to advance understanding these critical aerospace phenomena.
