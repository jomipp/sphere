Teaching Activities

Fluid Mechanics I (Undergraduate Level)

This course introduces the fundamentals of fluid mechanics and is given in the aerospace engineering degree (GIA) at ETSIAE. Topics covered include:

  • Kinematics of fluid motion
  • General equations of fluid motion
  • Fluid statics
  • One-dimensional fluid flow
  • Dimensional analysis and physical similarity
  • Ideal fluid flow

Fluid Mechanics II (Undergraduate Level)

This course builds upon the concepts introduced in Fluid Mechanics I and is given in the aerospace engineering degree (GIA) at ETSIAE. This course explores advanced topics such as:

  • Shock-waves
  • Irrotational flows (Prandtl-Meyer discontinuities)
  • Transient flows in pipelines
  • Hydrodynamic lubrication
  • Fluid flow in porous media
  • Theory of the laminar boundary layer

Computational Fluid Dynamics (Undergraduate Level)

This course introduces students to the fundamentals of computational fluid dynamics (CFD), and is given in the aerospace engineering degree (GIA) at ETSIAE. Topics include:

  • Introduction to CFD and its applications
  • Spatial and temporal integration methods
  • One-dimensional Euler equations
  • Two-dimensional flow formulations
  • One and two-dimensional acoustic equations
  • Acceleration methods
  • Resolution of elliptic problems
  • Introduction to Finite Volumen Method

Advanced Computational Fluid Mechanics (Master's Level)

This advanced course focuses on the application of computational methods to ideal fluid flows i n aerospace systems. This course is given at the UPV/EHU.
